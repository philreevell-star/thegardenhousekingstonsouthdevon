One Bedroom Holiday Cottage in Kingston, South Devon

The Garden House is a charming one bedroom self-catering holiday cottage in Kingston, a village in the South Hams district of South Devon. It is ideal for couples looking for a peaceful retreat with easy access to the South Devon Coastal Path and some of the region's finest beaches. Kingston is a quiet, unspoilt village set within an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty (AONB), making it one of the most sought-after locations for a holiday cottage in South Devon.

Guests staying at this one bedroom holiday cottage near Bigbury-on-Sea can explore Wonwell Sands beach (1.5 miles), the secluded Ayrmer Cove (2.5 miles), and the iconic Burgh Island — famous for its Art Deco hotel and tidal causeway. The market town of Kingsbridge, the sailing haven of Salcombe, and the historic port of Dartmouth are all within comfortable driving distance.

Whether you're looking for a short break walking the South West Coast Path, a romantic couples retreat, or simply a quiet holiday cottage in South Devon with a beautiful garden setting, The Garden House in Kingston offers a genuinely special experience.
