One Bedroom Holiday Cottage in Kingston, South Devon

The Garden House is a charming one bedroom self-catering holiday cottage in Kingston, a village in the South Hams district of South Devon. It is ideal for couples looking for a peaceful retreat with easy access to the South Devon Coastal Path and some of the region's finest beaches. Kingston is a quiet, unspoilt village set within an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty (AONB), making it one of the most sought-after locations for a holiday cottage in South Devon.

Guests staying at this one bedroom holiday cottage near Bigbury-on-Sea can explore Wonwell Sands beach (1.5 miles), the secluded Ayrmer Cove (2.5 miles), and the iconic Burgh Island — famous for its Art Deco hotel and tidal causeway. The market town of Kingsbridge, the sailing haven of Salcombe, and the historic port of Dartmouth are all within comfortable driving distance.

Whether you're looking for a short break walking the South West Coast Path, a romantic couples retreat, or simply a quiet holiday cottage in South Devon with a beautiful garden setting, The Garden House in Kingston offers a genuinely special experience. What’s nearby

The Garden House sits at the heart of some of South Devon’s most beautiful coastline and countryside. Here’s a taste of what’s on your doorstep.

Estuary  ·  1.5 miles

Wonwell & the Erme Estuary

A lovely walk over fields from The Garden House brings you to the Erme estuary at Wonwell in around 50 minutes — or five minutes by car. Rocky outcrops, breaking waves and rolling Devon fields make this one of the most peaceful stretches of this coast.

Beach  ·  2.5 miles

Ayrmer Cove

A secluded cove tucked beneath dramatic cliffs, reached on foot via the South West Coast Path. One of the most peaceful and unspoilt spots on the South Devon coast.

Landmark  ·  5 miles

Burgh Island

Walk across the sand at low tide or take the famous sea tractor at high tide to this iconic tidal island, home to a magnificent Art Deco hotel and the ancient Pilchard Inn. Even better at sunset.

Walking  ·  On the doorstep

South West Coast Path

England’s longest national trail passes right through this stretch of coast. Step out from Kingston and within minutes the path opens onto views like this — Bigbury beach sweeping below, Burgh Island on the horizon, and the whole South Hams coast stretching ahead.

Town  ·  13.5 miles

Salcombe

One of Devon’s most beautiful estuaries — great restaurants, independent shops, ferry trips to South Sands, and views like this around every corner.

Town  ·  20 miles

Dartmouth

A historic port town full of life — medieval castle, steam railway, exceptional restaurants and a harbour that draws everything from tall ships to the occasional naval frigate. Best visited on a long summer evening.

Beach  ·  4 miles

Westcombe Cove

A wild and unspoilt cove framed by dramatic striped cliffs, reached on foot via the coast path. No road access keeps it beautifully quiet — one of South Devon’s hidden gems.
